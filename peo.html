Program Educational Objectives - Electronics and Computer Engineering

Leadership

Graduates are Electronics and Computer Engineers occupying positions requiring
highly technical expertise in specialized fields of electronics engineering imbibed
with responsible leadership and ethical values..

Life-Long Learning

Graduates demonstrate life-long learning and continuous professional and career
development while practicing their profession

Social Responsibilities

Graduates will demonstrate service for the community and society that helps
uplift the lives of the people and will manifest concern for the environment.
